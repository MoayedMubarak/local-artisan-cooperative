Who We Are

A Marketplace Built on Craftsmanship

Founded in 2020, ArtsyVibe is a curated online marketplace dedicated to celebrating handcrafted goods from independent artisans around the world. We bridge the gap between skilled creators and conscious consumers who value authenticity over mass production.

From hand-thrown ceramics and woven textiles to carved woodwork and silver jewelry — every item on our platform is unique, thoughtfully made, and carries the fingerprint of the person who created it.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Authentic Craftsmanship

Every product on ArtsyVibe is handmade. We carefully vet each artisan to ensure quality and authenticity — no mass-produced imitations, ever.

Fair Artisan Earnings

We keep our commission low so artisans earn what they deserve. We believe creators should be the primary beneficiaries of their own work.

Sustainable Practices

We encourage artisans to use natural, locally-sourced, and recycled materials — building a marketplace that's kind to the planet.

Cultural Preservation

We celebrate heritage crafts from around the world — helping keep ancient techniques alive by connecting them with modern audiences.

Buyer Protection

Shop with confidence. Our buyer guarantee ensures you receive exactly what you ordered — or your money back, no questions asked.

Community First

We foster a community where artisans support each other and customers become long-term patrons — not just one-time buyers.
