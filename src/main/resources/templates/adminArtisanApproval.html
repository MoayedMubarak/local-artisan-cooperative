Shop Information

The Clay Studio

A boutique ceramics studio focused on creating functional art for everyday use. Each piece is hand-thrown and glazed with natural, food-safe materials. We believe in slow, intentional craftsmanship.

Biography

I am a passionate ceramics artist with over 10 years of experience creating functional and decorative pottery. My journey began in college when I took my first pottery class and immediately fell in love with the meditative process of working with clay.

My work focuses on sustainable practices and natural glazes inspired by the Pacific Northwest landscape where I grew up. I source local clays whenever possible and fire my kiln using renewable energy. Each piece tells a story of the earth it came from and the hands that shaped it.

I specialize in wheel-thrown vessels, from intimate tea cups to large serving bowls. My aesthetic is minimalist yet warm, with glazes that evoke the colors of forest moss, ocean waves, and desert sunsets.
